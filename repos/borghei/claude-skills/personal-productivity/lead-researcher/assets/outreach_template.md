# Cold Outreach Template — Signal-Led First Touch

Use one signal. Keep it under 90 words. End on a single, specific question.

---

## Email Skeleton

**Subject:** `{specific noun, not a pitch}` — short, lowercase, no clickbait

> Examples:
> - "checkout abandonment ideas"
> - "your Snowflake setup"
> - "thoughts on your hiring spike"

**Body:**

```
Hi {first name},

{One sentence referencing the actual signal — e.g., funding round, hiring,
public quote, integration adoption, product launch. Be specific enough that
it could not have been sent to anyone else.}

{One sentence connecting that signal to the pain you solve. Use language they
would use, not your product's language.}

{One question — concrete, low-cost to answer, not "do you want to chat?"}

— {Your name}
{One line of credentials, e.g., "previously @ Stripe / led billing infra"}
```

---

## Worked Example

> Subject: your Snowflake migration
>
> Hi Priya,
>
> Saw your post about migrating the analytics stack to Snowflake last month — congrats on shipping that. We've helped four data teams in similar moves cut warehouse spend 30-40% by tuning warehouse sizing and query routing in the first 90 days post-migration.
>
> Curious — what's been the biggest unexpected cost line item since you cut over?
>
> — Mo
> previously @ Mode Analytics, now building cost-tuning tooling for Snowflake teams

## Anti-Patterns

| Don't | Why |
|-------|-----|
| "Hope you're doing well!" | Filler. Skip pleasantries. |
| "I came across your profile" | Too generic. Replace with the actual signal. |
| Multi-paragraph value-prop dump | Reads as a deck. The first email should ask one thing. |
| "Do you have 15 min to chat?" | Too high-cost an ask. Replace with a question they can answer in one line. |
| Three CTAs | Pick one. Three is none. |
| Image attachments / logos | Increases spam rate, doesn't help reply rate. |

---

## Sequence Cadence (lightweight)

| Day | Touch | Channel |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 0   | Signal-led first email (this template) | Email |
| 3   | Brief follow-up — one new angle, no "just checking in" | Email |
| 7   | LinkedIn connection request — reference the email subject | LinkedIn |
| 14  | Final email — short, gives them an out ("happy to deprioritize for now") | Email |

If no reply by day 21, drop and recycle in 90 days only if signals refresh.
